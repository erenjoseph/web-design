Kollam - The Hidden Paradise of Kerala

Welcome to Kollam

Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a picturesque coastal city in the southern state of Kerala, India. Known for its serene backwaters, lush green landscapes, and tranquil beaches, Kollam is often considered a hidden paradise for travelers seeking peace and natural beauty.

Why Visit Kollam?

  • Backwaters: Experience a peaceful boat ride through Ashtamudi Lake and other scenic waterways.
  • Beaches: Relax at Kollam Beach, Thirumullavaram Beach, and enjoy the calm Arabian Sea.
  • Culture & Heritage: Visit historical temples, churches, and enjoy traditional Kerala art forms.
  • Local Cuisine: Taste delicious seafood and authentic Kerala dishes.

Perfect for a Peaceful Escape

Whether you are an adventurer, a honeymooner, or someone seeking peace and quiet, Kollam has something to offer. With welcoming locals and untouched natural beauty, it's a destination that touches the soul.
